## Full Description (4000 chars max)
Nagme is a professional chromatic tuner designed for musicians. It instantly detects the note you play through your microphone and shows tuning accuracy in cents.

SUPPORTED INSTRUMENTS
- Violin
- Guitar (Standard, Drop D, Open G, DADGAD, Half Step Down, Open D)
- Bass Guitar (Standard, Drop D, Half Step Down)
- Ukulele (Standard, Low G, D Tuning)
- Baglama (Standard, Bozuk, Misket, Mustezat tunings)
- Oud
- Viola
- Cello
- Chromatic mode (all notes)

FEATURES
- High-precision YIN pitch detection algorithm
- Real-time frequency analysis (44100 Hz, 4096 buffer)
- Instrument-specific harmonic correction
- Alternative tuning presets (12+ presets)
- Reference tone generator (3 waveforms: sine, warm, bright)
- Adjustable reference frequency (415-466 Hz)
- Sensitivity control
- Tuning session history
- Dark and light themes
- Turkish and English language support
- Fully offline — no internet required

PROFESSIONAL DESIGN
- Spring physics gauge with smooth needle movement
- Strobe display for precise tuning
- Haptic feedback
- Glassmorphic UI design

Nagme is designed for those who need a reliable tuning tool backstage or during practice at home. Completely free, no ads.
